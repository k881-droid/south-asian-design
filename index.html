Research Guide: South Asian Design

An internet archive of South Asian design.

  1. @architecturebyari

    Centered around the diaspora. Focuses on a stream of designs that showcase tradition, nostalgia, and a longing for the homeland. Elements of South Asian graphic design and architecture.

  2. @diet_paratha

    Digital South Asian owned agency. Discusses current design trends (digital and non-digital) from the context of South Asia. Also comments on misuse of South Asian design/ lack of credit.

  3. @the.olio.stories

    Jewelry brand based in India. Pieces crafted around Indian motifs and age-old subcontinental jewelry-making practices. Traditional medium, yet digital design is woven inton every campaign. Use of South Asian motifs in the digital landscape and branding.

  4. @harisism_

    Designer from Islamabad, Pakistan. Currently building his design language @ UAL . Explores design, language and culture within the South Asian context.

  5. @deconstruction_station

    Pakistan-based illustrator and animator. Daily/weekly illustrations of everyday vehicles within the Pakistani landscape. Common favorite is the rickshaw.

  6. @asim_m01

    Designer from Karachi, Pakistan. Explores desi typography, visuals and motion design.

  7. @areebtariq111

    Designer from Karachi, Pakistan. Founder of ajeeb studio, a creative branding studio. Explores South-asian typography and motion design.

  8. @prachipop.studio

    Designer and content creator based in India. Commentary on current South-asian design practices in the global context. Not just digital design, but hand-crafted designs, textiles, and fashion.

  9. @muhammedsajid.n

    Illustrator and visual designer from the subcontinent. Explores detailed imagery of South Asian archetypes, displaying them in a new light.

  10. @umairnajeebkhan

    Pakistani illustrator and animator. Focuses on displaying cultural moments through detail and color. Has helped bring illustration into the Pakistani advertising lanscape.

  11. @beygumbano

    Visual designer from Lahore, Pakistan. Experimental digital and print design in the desi context. Often uses ancient old desi motifs in a contemporary context; e.g. the tiger.

  12. @hashimali90

    Lahore-based art director. While not necessarily digital design, Ali's legacy is deep-rooted in South Asian aesthetics and history. Should be studied to further include these elements in the South Asian digital landscape.

  13. @mahoor_jamal

    Illustrator and art director from Pakistan. Focuses on digitalizing South Asian clothing and hyper-feminity.
